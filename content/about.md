## The Story Behind the Name

I was once introduced as "Medya-Medya"<br>
—a play on my role in media and<br>
a challenge about being "halfway" in my walk with the Lord.

It stayed with me.<br>
Not as a weight, but as a wake-up call.

**Hindi Media Media** was born from that moment. It’s my way of saying: I’m not doing this by halves anymore.<br>
Whether I’m clicking a lyric slide, editing a video, or managing a page, it’s all-out service.

It’s not just media. It’s a ministry of wholeness. 🤍
